Once upon a time there lived a young girl, called Cinderella. She had a step-mother and two ugly step-sisters, who were very unkind to her. One day, a prince invited them to a ball. The ugly sisters went, but Cinderella had to stay at home. As she was sitting by the fireside crying, her fairy god-mother suddenly appeared before her.
